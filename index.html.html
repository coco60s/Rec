Our Story

Our journey begins in the vineyards of Mendoza, where Paulina, a devoted mother, has been cultivating grapes for over two decades. She loves her work and tends to her vines with great care. Once the grapes are fully ripe and harvested, her young uncle Carlos—who runs a local juicing operation—takes over from there.

Sustainability Efforts

Farmers in Mendoza are increasingly feeling the effects of global warming, with rising annual temperatures and declining crop yields. Their challenges are further compounded by pollution from nearby industrialized districts. To help address the situation, Island Wine Works is reinvesting profits from its Malbec kits into reforestation efforts across the Mendoza region.
